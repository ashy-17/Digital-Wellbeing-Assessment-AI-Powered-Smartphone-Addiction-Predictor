Welcome to the Digital Wellbeing Predictor

Before you take the assessment, we want to be transparent about how our AI was trained. Initially, our dataset had a severe imbalance (too many addicted profiles, too few non-addicted). To ensure fair and accurate predictions, we applied Over-Sampling to balance the data.

Our model now achieves 92.47% accuracy using a balanced Random Forest Classifier.
